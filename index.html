What classes does the academy offer?
▼

What is the batch size?
▼
We maintain small batches to ensure every student gets personal attention. Our teachers know every student by name and track individual progress closely. This is one of our core strengths.

▼
We have morning batches from 7:00 AM to 9:00 AM and evening batches from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. Special Sunday doubt-clearing sessions are also available for students who need extra support.
